We are building SpenVest — a modern way to connect everyday spending with long-term financial wellbeing.

SpenVest Pty Ltd is an early-stage fintech company based in the Australian Capital Territory. Our work focuses on developing technology that helps people get more long-term value from the money they already spend, through responsible automation and clear, user-friendly experiences.

At this stage we are not publicly disclosing our internal system design, algorithms or reward structures. This website exists only to confirm our identity, outline our focus at a high level, and provide a point of contact while we continue research, prototyping and regulatory planning.
